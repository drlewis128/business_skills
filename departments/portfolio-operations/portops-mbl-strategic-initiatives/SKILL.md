# MBL Strategic Initiatives Tracker

MBL HoldCo's AI transformation depends on three interconnected initiatives — the AI Orchestration roadmap (Phases 0-4 via Krista.ai), the Meeting Intelligence System (weekly summaries and pre-meeting prep), and the Portfolio Skills Library — each of which must stay on track at MBL before it can be extended to Allevio, HIVE Partners, or Column6. This skill tracks milestone velocity, initiative health scoring, ROI metrics, and phase gate readiness for Dr. Lewis monthly and Matt Mathison weekly during active initiative periods.

## When to Use

- Monthly to compile Dr. Lewis's initiative progress report before Matt's briefing
- Weekly during active initiative phases when Matt is expecting progress updates
- Before any phase gate decision (e.g., Phase 0 to Phase 1 readiness check)
- When a milestone slips and a revised timeline needs to be communicated to Matt
- To assess portfolio entity readiness before extending an MBL initiative to Allevio, HIVE, or Column6

## Output Contract

- Phase gate decisions are not automatic based on milestone completion — they require Matt Mathison's explicit written sign-off because each phase authorizes additional Krista.ai integration scope, budget commitment, and organizational change for portfolio companies. The tracker surfaces phase readiness (all milestones green, no blockers) as a recommendation, but never advances a phase status to the next level without Matt's documented approval. Informal verbal approval noted in a meeting does not count; a written confirmation via Outlook or Teams message is required and logged.

- ROI tracking uses three distinct value dimensions — time saved, cost avoided, and revenue enabled — because a single composite metric obscures whether value is being created through efficiency, financial discipline, or growth, which matter differently to Matt as a PE operator. All three dimensions must trend positively for an initiative to maintain GREEN ROI status; a RED reading in any single dimension triggers a review conversation with Matt even if the aggregate ROI calculation appears positive, because the dimension that is RED often signals a structural problem the aggregate masks.

- Milestone velocity below 80% (milestones completed on schedule divided by milestones planned) triggers a revised timeline notification to Matt rather than an informal catch-up plan, because slippage at the HoldCo level creates cascading delays for portfolio entity rollouts — if Phase 3 slips at MBL, the Phase 4 Allevio rollout cannot begin on its planned date, which then flows through to Allevio's operational improvement timeline and potentially their management fee justification. Early notification at the 80% threshold preserves Matt's ability to reprioritize resources or adjust scope before the portfolio impact is felt.

**Reads from:** Monday.com (milestone boards, task completion status, initiative health cards); SharePoint (initiative specs, phase gate documents, meeting intelligence output archive, skills registry); Krista.ai platform (workflow run counts, adoption metrics, pipeline success rates); portops-mbl-system-ops (connector health data for initiative dependency verification)
**Writes to:** Monday.com (initiative health cards, milestone completion updates, blocker tasks for Matt decision); Dr. Lewis weekly digest; Matt Mathison weekly strategic update via Outlook; quarterly initiative review package via SharePoint
**HITL Required:** Matt Mathison approves all phase gate advancements in writing before status advances; Dr. Lewis validates ROI methodology and figures before each quarterly Matt report; entity CEOs confirm deployment readiness before portfolio rollout milestones are marked complete in the tracker

## Test Cases

- **Golden path:** All phases on schedule; Phase 2 meeting intelligence workflows running at >95% delivery rate; Matt's prep sheet adoption at 85%; skills library growing; all ROI metrics positive at >3x — monthly report generates with all-green status and clean milestone summary requiring no Matt decisions.
- **Edge case:** A Phase 2 milestone (Monday.com push automation) is marked complete in Monday.com but the portops-mbl-system-ops skill shows the Krista.ai to Monday.com connector at 72% success rate for the past 7 days — skill must cross-validate Monday.com task status against connector health data and reclassify the milestone as YELLOW pending resolution, not accept the Monday.com completion flag at face value.
- **Adversarial:** Matt verbally approves the Phase 1 to Phase 2 gate in a Monday standing meeting and tells Dr. Lewis to proceed — skill must flag Phase 2 status as PENDING WRITTEN APPROVAL and hold the phase gate open until a written confirmation (Outlook reply or Teams message) is received and archived. The tracker must not advance Phase 2 to active status based on verbal approval, regardless of urgency or Matt's direct instruction in conversation.

## Audit Log

All phase gate decisions are logged with decision date, Matt's approval medium (Outlook email, Teams message, signed document), approver identity, and the milestone checklist state at the time of approval. ROI figures are archived monthly with the methodology version, data sources, and calculation assumptions so figures can be recreated and audited during LP reporting or exit due diligence. Milestone velocity calculations are retained with the full milestone list and completion dates for each reporting period. Blocker resolutions are logged with resolution date, responsible party, and the decision or action that resolved the blocker.

## Deprecation

This skill is superseded when MBL HoldCo adopts a formal project portfolio management platform such as Planview or Smartsheet Portfolio with automated milestone tracking, health scoring, and ROI dashboards connected to Monday.com and Krista.ai data. At that point, the phase milestone tracking and velocity calculation sections are replaced by platform exports, and this skill retains only the Matt approval gate logic, THRIVE alignment check, and portfolio entity readiness criteria. Archive all historical phase gate decisions, ROI actuals, and milestone records before decommissioning — these records are material for LP reporting and exit documentation and must be retained for at least 7 years.
