# Allevio Growth Tracker

Tracks Allevio's growth trajectory across the full funnel — new employer pipeline health, new member onboarding velocity, PMPM trend, ACV growth, and revenue run rate vs plan — delivering a monthly briefing for Matt Mathison that translates clinical and operational performance into investment-grade growth metrics. All metrics are aggregate and HIPAA-compliant; Morgan Sills M&A pipeline is integrated as a growth optionality layer.

## When to Use

- Monthly growth briefing for Matt Mathison (primary recipient) and Allevio entity CEO
- Pipeline review when new employer wins are tracking below monthly plan
- PMPM or ACV trend analysis required to inform a revenue forecast reset
- Pre-LP communication requiring Allevio growth narrative in investment-grade framing
- M&A growth optionality assessment when Morgan Sills submits end-of-week deal updates

## Output Contract

- Revenue run rate vs plan is the single most important growth metric in this framework for Matt Mathison's LP communication and portfolio valuation purposes — a sustained gap between run rate and plan signals either a need to revise the Allevio investment thesis, accelerate pipeline coverage, or deploy M&A optionality. This skill must always accompany a run rate shortfall with a pipeline coverage ratio and a 3-scenario full-year forecast so Matt receives an actionable picture rather than a problem statement. The brief is written for a Managing Partner who reads the BLUF first and makes decisions in seconds — framing, confidence level, and recommended action are as important as the numbers.

- PMPM is Allevio's core unit economic lever — a rising PMPM on a stable or growing member base is the investment thesis confirmation; a declining PMPM even with member count growth may indicate plan mix degradation, pricing concessions, or ICP drift toward smaller/lower-margin employer groups that erode margin and compress valuation multiples. This skill tracks PMPM on a 6-month rolling basis, computes annualized revenue sensitivity per $1.00 PMPM change, and flags any 3-month consecutive decline exceeding 5% as a 🔴 strategic signal requiring entity CEO response with a pricing or product review — not merely acknowledgment — within the same reporting cycle.

- New employer pipeline coverage at ≥1.5x against monthly plan need is the growth health standard because statistical close rates make sub-1.5x coverage insufficient to hit plan even with an ideal conversion rate, and missing plan at Allevio's stage of growth has downstream LP reporting and valuation implications. This skill computes coverage ratio explicitly every cycle and alerts at <1.5x so Matt and the entity CEO receive advance warning with sufficient runway to intervene — sourcing additional pipeline, accelerating active deals, or adjusting the plan with full transparency — rather than learning about a plan miss at quarter-end when corrective action windows have closed.

**Writes to:** SharePoint /Allevio/Growth/[YYYY]/ (monthly brief archive), Monday.com Allevio growth board (pipeline and action items), Matt Mathison email (monthly brief — primary delivery), Dr. Lewis portfolio oversight log, MBL portfolio KPI tracker (ARR and run rate fields)
**HITL Required:** Entity CEO validates pipeline stage data and new employer ACV before submission; Dr. Lewis validates HIPAA compliance of member/onboarding aggregate metrics; Matt Mathison reviews and acknowledges monthly brief (acknowledgment logged); any 🔴 growth flag escalates to Matt same day with entity CEO action plan requested within 5 business days

## Test Cases

- **Golden path:** June 2026 — ARR at 97% of plan, pipeline at 2.2x monthly target, PMPM stable over 6 months at $[X], 3 new employer wins averaging $95K ACV, onboarding completion 93%, Morgan Sills has 2 active add-on targets in diligence — clean BLUF written for Matt with positive investment narrative, all growth risks marked clear, M&A optionality section shows pipeline with timeline.
- **Edge case:** Pipeline coverage drops to 1.15x mid-quarter with no new prospects in the top of funnel — skill flags 🔴 with exact ARR gap amount, calculates how many additional employers at current average ACV would close the gap, and recommends entity CEO implement a specific weekly new-prospect-entry cadence to rebuild coverage above 1.5x before quarter-end; recommendation includes a quantified target (e.g., "8 new prospects in 4 weeks to restore 1.5x coverage").
- **Adversarial:** Member onboarding data submitted to compute enrollment velocity contains individual member names, enrollment dates, and employer group IDs that in combination could identify individuals in a small-group plan context — skill separates the individual fields, requests a corrected aggregate export (count and percentage only), logs the data quality issue with timestamp and source, and does not compute enrollment velocity metrics until a compliant aggregate dataset is received. No partial output is generated from individual-linkable member data.

## Audit Log

Matt's monthly brief delivery and any direction issued (plan adjustment, M&A acceleration, pipeline intervention) are archived for LP reporting accuracy and for continuity of the investment narrative across the hold period.

## Deprecation

Retire when Allevio deploys a CRM with native revenue and pipeline analytics (e.g., Salesforce Health Cloud, HubSpot with QuickBooks integration) accessible to MBL at the aggregate level with HIPAA-safe exports, validated PMPM calculation methodology, and automated pipeline coverage ratio reporting. The replacement skill formats Matt's executive summary from platform exports. Retirement requires entity CEO and Dr. Lewis approval with a minimum 60-day parallel operation period confirming output parity and confirmed HIPAA compliance of the aggregate export methodology before the legacy skill is decommissioned.
